Privacy
Policy

Last Updated: 01 March 2025

Jobey operates as a digital job marketplace connecting recruiters and applicants for short-term job engagements within India, currently operating in Chennai, Tamil Nadu.

By accessing or using the Platform, you agree to the collection, use, and disclosure of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

🔒 No full Aadhaar stored
🚫 No data sold to third parties
💳 Secure payment processing
⚖️ Indian law compliant
Your Privacy Matters

Click any section below to read the full details.

Jobey collects personal information necessary to provide its services. This may include:

  • Full name and mobile number verified via OTP
  • Profile photograph
  • Work experience and skills
  • Business details (for recruiters)
  • Masked identification details such as the last four digits of Aadhaar or PAN for verification purposes
  • Bank account details or UPI information to facilitate payouts

Jobey does not store full Aadhaar numbers.

We also collect transaction-related and technical information including:

  • Payment amounts, timestamps, payout records, and payment gateway reference numbers
  • IP address and device information
  • Usage logs to improve platform functionality and prevent fraud

All payments are processed through authorised third-party payment gateways such as Cashfree or similar providers. Jobey does not store complete card numbers or sensitive banking credentials on its servers. Payment data is handled in accordance with the security standards of the respective payment processor.

Information collected is used to:

  • Facilitate job postings and applications
  • Process payments and payouts
  • Verify user identity
  • Resolve disputes
  • Prevent fraudulent activities
  • Improve user experience
  • Comply with applicable Indian laws

Jobey does not sell personal data to third parties.

User information is retained for as long as the account remains active or as required for legal compliance, dispute resolution, or fraud prevention. Users may request correction or deletion of their information by contacting us, subject to applicable legal retention requirements.
